This is a plain-English summary for developers. The legal terms are in

## What You Can Do For Free

✅ Free for individuals and small teams:

- Use Spendemon personally, including homelab and hobby use.
- Use Spendemon for education, learning, demos, and teaching.
- Use Spendemon for non-commercial projects.
- Use Spendemon internally at small organizations with fewer than 5 employees
  or fewer than 5 Kubernetes clusters.

## When You Need A Paid License

❌ A paid commercial license is required for companies using Spendemon
commercially.

Commercial use includes:

- running Spendemon in production for a for-profit company;
- using Spendemon to support business operations;
- using Spendemon for consulting, client delivery, managed services, SaaS, or
  another paid product or service.

🔓 Each released version of Spendemon converts to the MIT License 4 years after
that version is released.

Newer versions may still be under the BSL-style terms until their own change
date arrives.
