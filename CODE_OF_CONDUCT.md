# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project aims to be a respectful, welcoming, and constructive space for everyone who participates.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to interact in ways that support collaboration, learning, and healthy disagreement. We want Spendemon to be a place where people can ask questions, share ideas, and contribute without being harassed, excluded, or intimidated.

## Expected Behavior

When participating in this project, please:

- Be respectful in discussions, reviews, and issue threads
- Assume good intent, while still giving honest and constructive feedback
- Be open to different viewpoints, experiences, and skill levels
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Show patience when helping others and receiving feedback
- Keep criticism specific, actionable, and professional

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in this project:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language or conduct
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing or threatening to publish someone else's private information
- Repeated bad-faith arguments, hostility, or intimidation
- Sexualized language or unwanted sexual attention
- Any other conduct that a maintainer reasonably determines is inappropriate for the project

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and reviews
- Documentation and other written communication related to the project
- Community interactions that are directly connected to this project

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code of conduct, please report it to the project maintainer privately if possible rather than opening a public issue.

When making a report, include:

- What happened
- Where it happened
- When it happened
- Any relevant screenshots, links, or other context

All reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as carefully as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they determine is appropriate in response to unacceptable behavior, including:

- Requesting a change in behavior
- Editing, hiding, or removing comments or content
- Closing discussions, issues, or pull requests
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation

Enforcement decisions are made to protect the health of the project and its community.

## Final Notes

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this code of conduct and to respect maintainer decisions about community standards and enforcement.
